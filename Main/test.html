Cappuccino Recipe

If you want a delicious cappuccino but are unable to visit your local coffee shop, follow these instructions to prepare one at home. Savor and enjoy your freshly made cappuccino!

Ingredients

  • 2 shot of espresso
  • 180 ml cup of steamed milk
  • 1/3 froth
  • Cinnamon powder on top for garnish
  • Serve with sugar syrup on the side

    • Instructions

  • Brew a shot of espresso.
  • Steam the milk until it become foamy
  • Pour the hot espresso into a cup.
  • Pour the hot milk over the espresso.
  • Spoon the milk foam on top.
  • Sprinkle cinnamon powder on top for garnish.
  • Optional: Add sugar on the side

    • Enjoy your delightful cappuccino!
