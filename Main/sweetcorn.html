Prep: 12 mins | Cook: 13 mins | Servings: 4 to 6

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: Fusion

Savor the fusion of sweet corn pancakes and Mt. Tam Cheese—a delightful blend of sweet and creamy indulgence.

Credit: FOOD&WINE

Ingredients:

3 large ears of corn, shucked
1 large egg, lightly beaten
1 1/4 cups water
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup finely chopped scallions
1/4 cup canola oil, plus more as needed
2 ounces triple-cream cheese, cut into 1/2-inch pieces

Method:

Step 1

In a large saucepan of salted boiling water, cook the corn until crisp-tender, 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer to a plate and let cool completely, then cut the kernels off the cobs.

Step 2

In a large bowl, beat the egg with the water. Sift the flour, salt and baking powder over the egg and whisk until a batter forms. Stir in the corn kernels and scallions.

Step 3

In a large nonstick skillet, heat the 1/4 cup of canola oil until shimmering.

Step 4

Flip the pancake over using a metal spatula and cook for another minute or until golden. Place the pancake onto a plate and cover with a clean tea towel

Step 5

Flip the cakes and top each with a piece of cheese.

Step 6

Cook until the cheese just starts to melt and the corn cakes are cooked through, about 2 minutes longer. Transfer the corn cakes to a serving platter and season with black pepper.

Step 7

Repeat with the remaining batter. Serve warm.
