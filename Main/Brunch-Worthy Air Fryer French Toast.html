Prep: 5 min | Cook: 15 mins | Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: French

You may sprinkle the top with pecans if you wish.

Credit: allrecipes

Ingredients:

⅔ cup butter pecan-flavored coffee creamer
2 large eggs
1 pinch salt
4 slices brioche bread
nonstick cooking spray

Method:

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F (95 degrees C). Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 6 minutes.

Step 2

Whip together coffee creamer, eggs, and salt in a shallow bowl or deep plate. Dip each slice of brioche into the mixture, shaking off excess.

Step 3

Spray the air fryer basket with nonstick spray. Working in batches, place bread in the basket, making sure slices do not overlap.

Step 4

Reduce air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and fry French toast for 7 minutes, flipping at the halfway mark. Repeat with remaining bread.

Step 5

Keep cooked French toast warm in oven until batches are complete.
