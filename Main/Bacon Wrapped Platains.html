Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 35 mins | Servings: 18

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: Fusion

These bacon wrapped plantains are sweet, spicy and all things nice and I swear you guys are going to fall head over heels as soon as they pop out of the oven.

Credit: ambitious kitchen

Ingredients:

2 large dark spotted plantains,
12 ounces uncooked bacon
1-2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
Cayenne pepper, to season

Method:

Step 1

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with foil. Slice both plantains into 1/2 inch slices. Cut your strips of bacon in half.

Step 2

Take one plantain slice and wrap ½ piece of bacon around the flat side of the round. Repeat this.

Step 3

Place all bacon-wrapped plantains on your baking sheet seam side down.

Step 4

Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes until the bacon is crispy. Stick a toothpick in each bacon-wrapped plantain, place on a serving tray and enjoy! Serves 6.
