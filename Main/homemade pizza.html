Prep: 35 mins | Cook: 15 mins | Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: Italian

A homemade pizza, just like the name suggest it's a very easy recipe to make for your own pizza!

Credit: Ephraim ^-^

Ingredients:

Pizza Dough:

2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
1 teaspoon sugar
1 cup warm water (110°F/43°C)
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons olive oil

Pizza Sauce:

1 can (15 ounces) crushed tomatoes
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings:

Shredded mozzarella cheese
Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, sliced bell peppers,
onions, olives, mushrooms, etc.)

Method:

Prepare the Dough

Step 1

In a bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

Step 2

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil.

Step 3

Mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth.

Step 4

Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

Prepare the Pizza Sauce

Step 5

In a saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer over low heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Preheat the Oven

Step 6

Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C).

Shape and Assemble the Pizza

Step 7

Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

Step 8

Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet.

Step 9

Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

Step 10

Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your favorite toppings.

Bake the Pizza:

Step 11

Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 12

Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
