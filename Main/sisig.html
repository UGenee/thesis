Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 25 mins | Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: Filipino

Bangus sisig is a good version that can be considered as an alternative to pork. It is healthier, yummy, and easy to prepare.

Credit: Ephraim ^-^

Ingredients:

1 piece boneless bangus
1 piece white onion
1 Lemon (alternate: 2 Calamansi)
Mayonaise
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1 tablespoon cooking oil
2 Chili Pepper (Add or Lessen to your liking)
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 cloves of Garlic (Adjust to your liking)
Salt n' Pepper to taste

Method:

Step 1

Deep fry the bangus. Then flake once cold.

Step 2

Melt butter in a large pan

Step 3

Sauté the onion and garlic for 3 minutes.

Step 4

Add the chili and flaked fish.

Step 5

Put soy sauce and salt n' pepper then taste. (Adjust to your liking)

Step 6

Add Lime/Calamansi. (Can also mix the mayonaise from here then stir)

Step 7

Serve in a plate then garnish with mayonaise.
