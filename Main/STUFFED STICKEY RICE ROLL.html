Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 10 mins | Servings: 5

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: Chinese

The most appealing is the stuffed sticky rice balls (糯米饭团) with fried oil stick, pickles and meat sung. Really a great combination because the taste and texture is so rich in every bite.

Credit: China sichuan food

Ingredients:

2.5 cup sticky rice glutinous rice
1.25 cup white rice
3.75 cup water
5 pieces Chinese fried oil stick 油条
2.5 cup pork sung 肉松
toasted black sesame seeds
½ Chinese preserved vegetables

Method:

Step 1

Steam the rice

Step 2

Pre-soak sticky rice and white rice in clean water in the previous day and then drain.

Step 3

Set up a steamer with a clean cloth, then spread the rice evenly and steam for 20 minutes.

Step 4

Cut Takuan into long strips (around 1 cm in thickness) and then fry or toast the oil stick until they become crispy again.

Step 5

Place plastic wrapper on a sushi rolling mat, sprinkle some toasted black sesame seeds and then scoop out ½ of the rice and flatten into a thin layer. The rice can be quite sticky, so wet your hands when assembling.

Step 6

Spread a layer of pork sung and then two strips of Takuan on the two ends. Place one oil sticky in center and then roll up the filling in the center of the mat. Slightly press the roll. Repeat to finish the other half. Then take the mat off and cut the roll in half (with plastic wrapper on).

step 7

Serve hot and enjoy!
