Prep: 15 min | Cook: 20 mins | Servings: 6

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: French

This almond French toast recipe is perfect for Mother's Day! It's a nice twist to the traditional breakfast dish and makes a nice addition to any brunch.

Credit: allrecipes

Ingredients:

1 cup slivered almonds
3 eggs
1 cup milk
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
¼ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon almond extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
12 thick slices French bread
3 tablespoons canola oil
3 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar for dusting, or as needed (Optional)

Method:

Step 1

Toast almonds in a small saucepan over low heat, tossing frequently, until lightly browned, 5 to 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and set aside.

Step 2

In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, flour, salt, baking powder, almond extract, and vanilla extract. Soak bread slices in the mixture until saturated, then transfer to a shallow pan. Cover and refrigerate for approximately 1 hour.

Step 3

Heat canola oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Press one side of each soaked bread slice into toasted almonds to coat. Fry bread slices on both sides until golden brown. Dust with confectioner's sugar before serving,
