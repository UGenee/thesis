Prep: 5 mins | Cook: 5 mins | Servings: 2

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: Australian

Make our classic smashed avocado toast recipe with poached eggs for an Australian café style breakfast at home

Credit: australian avocados

Ingredients:

2 slices sourdough, or your favourite bread, toasted
½ large ripe avocado
1 zest and juice from 1/2 lemon
1 small red chilli, sliced
fresh coriander or parsley leaves, torn
salt & pepper

Method:

Step 1

Smash avocado in a small bowl. Stir through lemon juice, chilli and herbs, season to taste with salt and pepper.

Step 2

2Spoon avocado over toast, serve immediately.

Step 3

Serve and enjoy!
