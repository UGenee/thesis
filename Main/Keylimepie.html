Prep: 20 mins | Cook: N/A mins | Servings: 8 Servings

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: American

You need only five ingredients to create this refreshing, easy Key lime pie. It's special enough for weekend potlucks, but easy enough to make for a weeknight dessert.

Credit: tasteofhome.com

Ingredients:

1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
(14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup Key lime juice or lime juice
1 graham cracker crust (9 inches)
2 cups whipped topping
Lime slices, optional

Method:

Step 1

In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth.

Step 2

Beat in milk and lime juice until blended. Pour into crust.

Step 3

Refrigerate, covered, at least 4 hours.

Step 4

Just before serving, garnish with whipped topping and, if desired, lime slices.
